#dairy/butter  #shortening  #sugar/brown  #sugar/molasses  #sugar/maple-syrup  #eggs  #flour/oat-flour  #baking-soda  #dessert  #seasonings/salt 

ingredients 1.5 cups butter or shortening 2 cups brown sugar 1/2 cup molasses or maple syrup 2 eggs 4.5 cups oat flour 4 tsp baking soda 1 tsp salt

instructions

  • cream butter, sugar, molasses, and egg until fluffy
  • sift together flour, soda, and salt
  • mix everything together. form small balls and roll in sugar
  • bake for 12 min in preheated oven
  • cool on drying rack for 10-15 min
  • spread [[cheem-cream]] lightly on two cookies and sandwich together

#dessert  #pie  #flour/all-purpose  #dairy/butter  #seasonings/salt 

ingredients

300 g    flour
13 g     sugar
5 g      sea salt
1 cup    cold butter
~ 1 cup  ice water

instructions

  • mix dry ingredients, kneed in butter
  • slowly add in water
  • roll out to 1/4" thick. save leftover for top
  • bake at 400 for 15 min
  • pull from oven, add pie crust weights, bake another 10-15 min

easter biscuits

#biscuits  #flour/soft-wheat  #flour/self-rising  #baking-powder  #breakfast  #brunch  #supper  #dairy/butter  #dairy/milk  #dairy/sour-cream  #dairy/yogurt  #dairy/buttermilk  #southern  #seasonings/salt  #seasonings/spices/black-pepper 

ingredients

2.5  cups    soft wheat self-rising flour
1    tsp     baking powder
1/4  tsp     salt
1/4  tsp     black pepper
1    tsp     sugar
1/4  cup     butter (half a stick)
1/2  cup     whole milk
1/2  cup     sour cream, buttermilk, or greek yogurt

instructions

  • mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  • kneed in softened butter, you’re making biscuits!
  • mix milk and sour in separate cup, save a little
  • stir with wooden paddle
  • drop 8-12 biscuits on parchment-lined pan, wash with leftover milk
  • bake for 12-15 minutes, turn halfway

notes

great as [[sausage biscuits]], [[biscuits & gravy]], or just served as a side with something like joshuas-favorite-chicken or veggie stew. you can also add 3/4 cup of chopped kielbasa with 3/4 cup of gouda for sausage & gouda biscuits.
