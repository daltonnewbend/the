There’s a kind of peace that comes in the autumn. The air smells clearer and feels crisp. Being swallowed up in a quilted duster made just for the wedding, and sipping good pour-over

from my #1 DAD mug. Computer stuff is super interesting to me. It’s fun to learn how to use the computer as much as possible without picking up the mouse. Of course there’s things that still require it, but programs like Obsidian and Neovim make it easier. It’s really the browser that is the only thing that makes it hard. Oh and that music player. I need a different one, I think. I’m still going through the manual little by little to be sure I don’t miss anything. I’m eager to get to the configuration part where I can tell this system to open different apps based on the key bindings and somehow get rid of the bullshit I don’t want like AI and apps that aren’t so much for me.
