yesterday

Mar 1, 2025

Since yesterday, I’ve been unable to explain what it is I mean to say. In fact, I’ve been saying things that I don’t mean to say at all.

I didn’t mean yesterday, I mean’t sometime in the last five days. I’m not sure exactly how this is happening. If I’ve come down with an illness of some kind, giving me brain fog, or if I should consider cutting back on coffee.
