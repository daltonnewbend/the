spring roll cashew sauce

Jan 1, 1

ingredients

2 tsp    smoked paprika
1 tsp    ginger powder
1 tsp    chopped onion
3 medium cloves garlic, chopped
1/2 cup  cashew butter
1/8 cup  rice vinegar
1/8 cup  honey
1/8 cup  orange juice
1/8 cup  less-sodium soy sauce
1 tsp    salt, to taste
1 tsp    black pepper
1 tsp    red pepper flakes

instructions

  • in a food processor, blend ginger, garlic, and onion until chopped finely
  • add other ingredients and blend until smooth

notes

this is based on a recipe from noah baerman.
