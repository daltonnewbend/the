cheem cream

Jan 1, 1

ingredients

226g    softened cream cheese
2 tbs   softened butter
100g    powdered sugar
1 tsp   vanilla
1 tsp   black pepper

instructions

  • mix ingredients together thoroughly

notes

great on oatmeal-cream-pies or [[shortbread-cookies]]. named by jade thornton when we lived together and i made it for [[brownies]]
