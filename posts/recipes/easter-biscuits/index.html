easter biscuits

Jan 1, 1

ingredients

2.5  cups    soft wheat self-rising flour
1    tsp     baking powder
1/4  tsp     salt
1/4  tsp     black pepper
1    tsp     sugar
1/4  cup     butter (half a stick)
1/2  cup     whole milk
1/2  cup     sour cream, buttermilk, or greek yogurt

instructions

  • mix dry ingredients in a bowl
  • kneed in softened butter, you’re making biscuits!
  • mix milk and sour in separate cup, save a little
  • stir with wooden paddle
  • drop 8-12 biscuits on parchment-lined pan, wash with leftover milk
  • bake for 12-15 minutes, turn halfway

notes

great as [[sausage-biscuits]], [[biscuits-&-gravy]], or just served as a side with something like joshuas-favorite-chicken or veggie-stew . you can also add 3/4 cup of chopped kielbasa with 3/4 cup of gouda for sausage & gouda biscuits.
