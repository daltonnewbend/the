pumpkin pie

Jan 1, 1

ingredients

425 g    roasted pumkin puree
400 g    caramelized milk
2        eggs
1        egg yolk
3 g      ground cinnamon
2 g      ground ginger
1 g      ground clove
6 g      vanilla
2 g      sea salt
1 g      fresh ground nutmeg

instructions

  • mix together puree, [[caramelized-milk]], eggs, and spices
  • pour into pie-crust
