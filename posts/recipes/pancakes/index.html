pancakes!

Jan 1, 1

ingredients

1.5  cups    self-raising flour
4    tbs     butter
1    tbs     sugar
1    tsp     baking powder
1            egg
1    cup     milk

instructions

  • combine dry ingredients
  • add softened butter, you’re making biscuits
  • add eggs and milk
  • cook on large buttered skillet or pan- a 1/3 cup works well for the size
