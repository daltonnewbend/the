veggie stew

Jan 1, 1

ingredients

2    cups    pork stock
1            quartered onion
1    30oz    can of diced tomatoes
1    cup     butter beans
2            cloves minced garlic
1            can green beans

instructions

  • add [[pork-stock]] to a large stock pot
  • add in all other ingredients
  • simmer for an hour or more
  • serve with a [[lunchroom-sandwich]], [[grilled-cheese]], or easter-biscuits
