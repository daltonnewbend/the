Common Wood-Pigeon

Jun 5, 2019

Giardino Bardini | IT-52

I encountered this bird during study abroad in Italy. I recorded lots of birdsong for a project on [[tesselates]] I was working on. These pigeons were common in gardens we visited.

I later re-encountered this bird on a trip to the UK to visit my friend, Racheal, where it was heard throughout the country, also common for gardens and wildlife areas.
