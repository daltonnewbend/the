European Greenfinch

Jul 6, 2025

Orto Botanico di Firenze | IT-52 Jupiter Artland | GB-SCT

I encountered this bird during study abroad in Italy. I recorded lots of birdsong for a project on [[tesselates]] I was working on.

I later re-encountered this bird on a trip to the UK to visit my friend, Rachael, where it was heard at Jupiter Artland and several other places. I consider this encounter my first ’life’ encounter.
