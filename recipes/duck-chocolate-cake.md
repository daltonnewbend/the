#### ingredients
	1.75 cups          all purpose flour
	0.75 cup           cocoa powder
	1.5  tsp           baking powder
	1.5  tsp           baking soda
	2    cup           white sugar
	1    tsp           salt
	2                  eggs
	1    cup           milk
	0.5  cup           vegetable oil\
	2    tsp           vanilla extract
	1    cup           boiling water


#### instructions
- mix dry ingredients
- add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla
- add boiling water, whisking to incorporate
- pour batter into cake pans
- bake for 35 minutes

#### notes
Kate made this for me for my 29th birthday. She dressed it with whipped cream and a chocolate whipped cream duck. A super light and airy cake.
