I’m starting to get the hang of this markdown thing.#

It’s interesting what you can do with tags, properties, and simpler formatting. I haven’t quite standardized yet how I want my notes to look and whatnot, but It’ll happen naturally as I go. Right now, I’m really gung-ho on getting my recipes organized and standardized. After that, I’d love to start experimenting with custom #CSS classes.

Also on my mind is this hair experiment I’m doing to myself.#

I’m deciding to forgo shampoo in favor of hair washes I can make at home. It seems there are a lot of recipes online, mostly popular in India. The one I made first has a lot of turmeric in it, and I didn’t think about its natural ability to dye things a bright yellow. Thankfully it washes right out.

I finally sent out the invite for the southern-ish artist group the other night.#

I asked each person to submit three group name ideas, and we will all vote together. Each person will fill out a bracket to get to a ‘winner’ per-say, then all of those will be voted on by the group. I’ve already begun compiling dedicated services, remitted services, and a cookbook! I also began writing my stance on Proton and why I believe it should be the main servicer we use for #email, #drive, etc.
